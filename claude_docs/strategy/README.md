# Strategy

Business and product direction documents.

## Contents

- **BUSINESS_PLAN.md** — Tier 1 strategic authority. Market analysis, competitive positioning, financial projections, go-to-market strategy, and locked business decisions.
- **roadmap.md** — Parallel-path product roadmap (CA/CB/CC/CD/P tracks). Guides feature prioritization and sprint planning.
- **COMPLETED_PHASES.md** — Historical record of 21 shipped phases. Reference for completed work and architectural decisions.
- **pricing-strategy.md** — Fee model (5%/7%), competitor benchmarks, revenue projections, and monetization options.

## Load Policy

Load when:
- Planning sprints or new features
- Evaluating business decisions or market positioning
- Reviewing competitor analysis or pricing strategy
- Referencing completed phase context

## What Does NOT Belong Here

- Implementation details (those go in operations/)
- Session logs or retrospectives (those go in logs/)
- One-time audit reports (those go in archive/)
- Code-level technical decisions (those go in STACK.md)
