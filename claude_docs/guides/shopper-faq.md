# Shopper FAQ

## How do I find estate sales near me?

Open the FindA.Sale app or website and use the map or search feature. By default, sales are sorted by proximity to your location. You can also filter by sale type (regular or auction), date range, or search for specific items or locations. Follow your favorite organizers to get notified when they post new sales.

## How do I search for specific items?

Use the search bar to type in what you're looking for—"vintage lamps," "china dinnerware," "oak furniture," etc. Results will show all matching items across current and upcoming sales in your area. You can also browse by category or use tags to narrow results.

## How does bidding on auction items work?

In an auction sale, items go to the highest bidder. You place a bid, and if someone outbids you, you'll receive a notification. Keep bidding until the auction ends, and the highest bid at closing time wins. Auction end times are clearly marked so you know when to stop bidding.

## What is a Hold/Reservation?

A hold (or reservation) lets you request that an item be set aside for you until the sale opens or for a set period. The organizer can accept or decline your hold request. Holds are useful for high-value or popular items you want to secure before the sale starts.

## How do I complete a purchase?

Add items to your cart, review your order, and proceed to checkout. You'll enter payment information and confirm your purchase. Once paid, the item is yours, and you'll receive instructions for pickup or delivery (depending on the organizer's policy).

## What payment methods are accepted?

FindA.Sale accepts all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) through our secure Stripe payment processor. We do not accept cash, checks, or other payment methods through the platform.

## What is Hunt Pass and how do I earn points?

Hunt Pass is our loyalty rewards program for active shoppers. Every purchase earns you Hunt Pass points based on spending. Accumulate points to unlock discounts on future purchases. You can view your balance and redemption options in your account settings.

## How do I follow a sale organizer?

On any sale detail page, click the "Follow" button to stay connected to that organizer. You'll receive notifications when they create new sales, and their listings will appear first in your search results. You can manage your followed organizers in your account preferences.

## How do push notifications work?

If you enable notifications in your account settings, you'll be alerted when sales you're watching are about to start, when popular items are running low, or when organizers you follow post new sales. You can toggle notifications on or off at any time, or customize which types of alerts you receive.

## What is the return/refund policy?

Estate sale items are generally sold as-is without returns. However, if an item arrives damaged or doesn't match its description, contact the organizer immediately with photos. The organizer may offer a refund or replacement at their discretion. All refunds are processed back to your original payment method.

## How do I contact a sale organizer?

Click the organizer's name or profile on the sale page to open their profile. Look for a "Message" or "Contact" button to send them a direct message. Most organizers check messages regularly, so you should receive a reply within a few hours.

## How do I report a problem with an item?

If you purchase an item and discover a problem, contact the organizer directly through the messaging system with photos of the issue. Explain what's wrong and ask if they can offer a refund or replacement. If the organizer doesn't respond, you can report the issue to FindA.Sale support for assistance.
