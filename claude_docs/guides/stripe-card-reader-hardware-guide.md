# Stripe Card Reader Hardware for FindA.Sale POS

**Who this is for:** Organizers using the FindA.Sale Point of Sale (POS) feature to accept card payments at their sales.

---

## Quick Answer: Do I Need Hardware?

**No — not necessarily.** If you have a modern iPhone or Android phone, you can accept contactless card payments (tap-to-pay) without buying anything. See [Tap to Pay](#tap-to-pay-no-hardware-needed) below.

If you want a dedicated reader, the **Stripe Reader M2 ($59)** is the right choice for most organizers.

---

## The Readers

### Stripe Reader M2 — $59
**Best for: Most estate sale, yard sale, and flea market organizers**

- **How it connects:** Bluetooth to your phone or tablet
- **Accepts:** Tap (contactless/NFC), chip insert, and swipe
- **Size:** Pocket-sized, no screen
- **Power:** Built-in battery, charges via USB-C
- **Use it if:** You're moving around the sale, running multiple tables, or just getting started

The M2 is the workhorse for secondary sale organizers. It clips to your belt or drops in a pocket, pairs in seconds, and handles every card type your shoppers will bring.

**How to pair (Bluetooth):**
1. Charge the M2 fully before your sale day
2. Open the FindA.Sale POS on your phone
3. Go to POS Settings → Card Reader → Connect Reader
4. Hold the button on the M2 until the LED flashes — it will appear in the reader list
5. Tap to pair. The LED turns solid when connected.

---

### Stripe Reader S700 — $299
**Best for: Fixed checkout stations, higher-volume shops, antique/consignment stores**

- **How it connects:** WiFi (same network as your POS device) or Ethernet (with optional dock)
- **Accepts:** Tap, chip, swipe
- **Screen:** 5.5" color touchscreen — customers see the transaction and can sign/tip on screen
- **Use it if:** You have a dedicated checkout table, run a resale shop using Shop Mode, or want a professional-looking counter setup

The S700 runs on WiFi, so your phone and the reader just need to be on the same network. No cable between them. The customer-facing screen shows the item total and lets them tap/insert their card directly.

**Stripe Reader S710** is the same device with added **cellular (LTE) connectivity** — useful if your sale venue has unreliable WiFi. Also $299.

---

### BBPOS WisePOS E — $249
**Best for: Organizers who want a countertop reader but prefer the WisePOS E form factor**

- **How it connects:** WiFi or Ethernet (via optional $49 dock)
- **Accepts:** Tap, chip, swipe
- **Screen:** 5" color touchscreen
- **Use it if:** You already have one from a previous Stripe setup, or prefer this form factor over the S700

The WisePOS E is a solid reader but the S700 is the more current device. Either works fine with FindA.Sale POS.

## Tap to Pay — No Hardware Needed

If you have an **iPhone (iOS 16.7 or later)** or a **compatible Android phone**, you can accept contactless payments using only your device. The shopper taps their card, phone, or watch directly on your screen.

- **Cost:** $0 extra hardware cost
- **Accepts:** Contactless cards (tap), Apple Pay, Google Pay
- **Does NOT accept:** Chip insert or swipe
- **How to enable:** In FindA.Sale POS, go to POS Settings → Payment Method → Tap to Pay and follow the prompts

Tap to Pay is great for low-volume sellers or as a backup if your M2 battery dies mid-sale.

## Transaction Fees

All Stripe Terminal card readers use the same rate regardless of which reader you use:

**2.7% + 5¢ per successful transaction** (US cards)
**+1.5%** for international cards

There are no monthly fees or minimums for the hardware itself.

2. Go to **Terminal → Hardware**
3. Select the reader you want and add to cart

You can also order by contacting Stripe sales if you need multiple units.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does the M2 work without an internet connection?**
No — all Stripe Terminal readers require an internet connection to process payments. The M2 connects to your phone via Bluetooth, but your phone needs cellular data or WiFi to process the transaction.

**Can I use the same reader at multiple sales?**
Yes. Your reader is linked to your Stripe account, not a specific sale. Just bring it to the next event and connect it in the app.

**What if my reader battery dies during a sale?**
Switch to Tap to Pay in the FindA.Sale POS settings — this uses your phone directly and doesn't require the reader.

**Do I need a reader to use POS?**
No. The FindA.Sale POS also supports cash transactions and manual payment recording. Card readers are only needed if you want to accept card payments.

**Can multiple staff use the same reader?**
The reader connects to one device at a time. If you have multiple checkout points, you'll need a reader per station (or use Tap to Pay on each device).

**Is the M2 the same as the old Stripe Reader?**
The M2 replaced the original Stripe Reader. If you have an older Stripe reader, check if it's still supported in your Stripe Dashboard under Terminal → Hardware.
