# FindA.Sale Quick Start — 5 Easy Steps

## Step 1: Create Your Account

Visit **finda.sale** and click "Sign Up."

Enter your email and create a password. You'll get a confirmation email — click the link to confirm.

(You can also sign up with Google or Facebook if you want to skip the password.)

**Time:** 2 minutes

---

## Step 2: Set Up Stripe Payouts

After you log in, go to **Settings** → **Payout Method**.

Click "Connect with Stripe." You'll be asked for:
- Your business name
- Your personal name
- A checking account number (where your sales money goes)

Stripe will ask you a few questions to verify your identity. This usually takes 5–10 minutes.

Once approved, every sale payment goes straight to your bank account. We hold nothing.

**Time:** 10 minutes

---

## Step 3: Create Your First Sale

Click the **"Create Sale"** button on your dashboard.

Give your sale a name — use the street address or date. Example: "1234 Fulton Estate Sale – March 8-9"

Pick the sale dates and a start time.

Click **"Save Sale."**

Done. Your sale exists now.

**Time:** 2 minutes

---

## Step 4: Add Items (Manual + AI Tagging)

Click **"Add Items"** on your sale page.

**Option A: One item at a time**
- Take a photo or upload an existing one
- Type a short description (examples: "vintage lamp," "oak dresser," "box of books")
- Our AI fills in the category, condition, and a better description
- Review and accept or edit
- Click "Save"

**Option B: Bulk upload**
- Click "Upload Multiple" and add 5–10 items at once
- Type descriptions for each
- AI tags all of them together
- Review, accept, or edit in batch

**AI does the heavy lifting:**
- Automatically fills in the category (Furniture, Vintage, Tools, etc.)
- Suggests condition (Excellent, Good, Fair, Poor)
- Writes a better product description
- Suggests tags (Antique, Hand-painted, etc.)

You don't have to use the AI suggestions — edit anything you want.

**Time:** 10–15 minutes per 5 items

---

## Step 5: Publish and Share

Once you have at least 3–5 items added, click **"Publish Sale"** on your sale page.

Your sale is now live on FindA.Sale.

**Share it with:**
- Friends and family via text or email
- Your email list
- Facebook or Instagram
- Local buy/sell groups
- QR codes (print on signs or post online)

Click the **Share button** on your sale page to get the link and QR code.

Every view, click, and purchase shows up on your dashboard in real-time.

**Time:** 1 minute to publish, 5 minutes to share

---

## Extra Tips

### Pricing
- Use the pricing tool ($ button) on any item to see what similar items sold for recently
- Price higher if your item is rare or in excellent condition
- Lower price = faster sales (usually)

### Photos
- Clear, bright photos with good lighting sell faster
- Show the whole item from multiple angles if possible
- Close-ups help for damage or interesting details

### Descriptions
- Be specific: "1950s oak dresser with dovetail drawers" beats "old dresser"
- Mention any damage: "small scratch on top" is better than hiding it
- Keep it short — shoppers scan, they don't read essays

### When Items Sell
- You'll get a notification immediately
- The buyer will message you about pickup or shipping
- Once they pick it up or you ship it, mark it "Sold"
- Your payout happens automatically to your bank account
