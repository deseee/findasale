## Usage Notes

### Email Sending
- **List:** Use cold outreach contacts from `organizer-outreach.md` (customize [YOUR NAME] and [YOUR PHONE])
- **Timing:** Send Monday 9 AM for highest open rates
- **Follow-up:** If no reply after 3 days, send one gentle follow-up: "Just checking in — try FindA.Sale free for your next sale"
- **Bounce handling:** Remove bounced addresses from list immediately

### Social Media Posting
- **Timing:** Post at 9 AM on Monday (stagger across Instagram + Facebook by 10–15 minutes to avoid looking like bulk copy)
- **Tags:** Use hashtags from `marketing-content-2026-03-05.md#Hashtag Bank`
- **Pin:** Pin launch post to top of FindA.Sale business profile for 1 week
- **Engage:** Like + reply to all comments within 2 hours of posting

### Google Business Profile
- **Claim/Verify:** Ensure FindA.Sale business listing is verified before posting (if not already done)
- **Post scheduling:** Use Google Business web interface to schedule post
- **Weekly rotation:** After launch week, post a new update every Monday (cycle between new sales, organizer spotlights, and feature announcements)
- **Respond to reviews:** Check for reviews daily and respond within 24 hours

### Organic Amplification
- **Tag organizers:** If organizers have social media accounts, tag them in launch post (with permission)
- **Repost to stories:** Share launch post to Instagram Stories with swipe-up link (if account has Story links enabled)
- **Share across devices:** Encourage Patrick to share launch post to any personal networks / industry groups
- **Ask for shares:** In cold outreach email, ask organizers to share FindA.Sale with peers ("If you like it, share it — we grow by word-of-mouth")
