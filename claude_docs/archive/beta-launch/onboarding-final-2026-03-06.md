### FindA.Sale Quick Start — 10 Steps to Your First Live Sale

1. **Sign up at finda.sale** — Email, password, confirm. (2 min)

5. **Write quick descriptions** — One sentence per item: "oak dresser," "vintage lamp," etc. (5 min)

6. **Let AI tag** — Upload photos and descriptions. AI fills in category, condition, tags. (2 min)

7. **Review and save** — Check AI suggestions, edit any, save items. (5 min)

8. **Set prices** — Click $ icon to see comparable prices. Price fairly. (5 min)

9. **Publish sale** — Dashboard → "Publish Sale." Sale is now live. (1 min)

10. **Share everywhere** — Copy link, text friends, post on Facebook, print QR code. (5 min)

## Organizer FAQ (5 Common Questions)

These are questions organizers will ask during beta that the shopper FAQ doesn't cover.

### 1. How do I make sure my photos work with the AI tagging?

The AI works with almost any photo. For best results: take the photo in natural light, show the whole item, and get close enough that we can see details. If you have a vintage lamp, for example, show the full lamp from the side, then a close-up of the base or maker's mark. The AI reads all of it and writes a better description. You can always edit the AI's suggestions, so don't stress about perfect photos.

### 2. What happens if someone buys an item while I'm still adding more?

The item is immediately removed from sale and marked sold on the platform. No one else can buy it. You'll get a notification on your dashboard and an email with the buyer's info and pickup/shipping instructions. Mark it "Completed" once they pick it up or you ship it. If you're mid-sale and items are moving, you can pause the sale, add more inventory, and then re-publish. It's all live updates.

### 3. Can I run a regular sale and an auction at the same time?

Yes. You create one sale and mark individual items as either regular (fixed price) or auction. Auction items have a 7% fee instead of 5%. Auction end times are set per item, so you can have auctions ending at different times. Bidders get notified if they're outbid. When the auction ends, the highest bid wins and payment is processed to your Stripe account.

### 4. How much does FindA.Sale cost after beta?

During beta, it's free. After beta, we'll announce pricing. Most organizers will pay per-sale (5% on regular items, 7% on auction). We're keeping it simple—no monthly fee, just a small cut when you sell. Early beta organizers will get a special deal. If you have feedback on pricing, tell me.

### 5. What if I get stuck or have a technical problem during a sale?

Message me directly. I'm checking messages all day during beta, and I'll help troubleshoot or fix it live. Your sale is critical—I'm not waiting for support tickets. If something breaks, I'll either fix it or we'll work around it together. No organizer is left hanging during beta. That's the deal.
