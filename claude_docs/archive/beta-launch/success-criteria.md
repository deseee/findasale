# Beta Success Criteria

---

## Organizer Activation Milestones

1. **Registered account** — invite code redeemed
2. **Stripe Connect connected** — can receive payments
3. **First sale created (draft)** — used the product
4. **First sale published** — went live
5. **First 10 items listed** — meaningful inventory
6. **First buyer interaction** — view, save, or message
7. **First purchase completed** — full loop closed

---

## Beta Success Thresholds

**Target: 5 organizers, 4 weeks**

- **5/5 reach milestone 4 by week 2** = product is usable
- **3/5 reach milestone 7 by week 4** = core loop works
- **Avg NPS ≥ 7 after week 2** = retention signal

---

## Early Warning Signals

- **Organizer stuck at milestone 2** (Stripe) → intervene within 24h
- **No buyer activity on published sale after 3 days** → check map/search visibility
- **Support ticket within 48h of signup** → onboarding friction point
