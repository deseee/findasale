## 2. Beta Welcome Email

**From:** support@finda.sale
**Subject:** Welcome to FindA.Sale Beta — Here's how to get started

---

Welcome to FindA.Sale! We're thrilled to have you here.

You're now part of a small group of organizers in [City], [State] helping us shape the platform. Everything you see is still in beta — and that means you can try it completely free with no platform fees charged.

**What you can do right now:**

Create a complete sale listing in minutes. Add items one at a time or in batches, and our AI tagging system fills in descriptions and categories automatically. Publish your sale with a shareable link and start accepting online payments. You'll see exactly what sold, what you made, and which items are still waiting.

**Three things to try first:**

1. **Create a sale** — Click "New Sale" and give it a name and date. Takes 2 minutes.
2. **Add 5 items** — Take photos or use existing ones. Type a brief description — our AI does the rest.
3. **Share the link** — Copy your sale URL and send it to friends or post it online. You'll get live updates as people browse and bid.

**Where to get help:**

If something isn't clear, reply to this email or head to our guide at `/guide` on the site. We read every message and respond fast.

**Your feedback shapes what comes next:**

During beta, we're focused on speed and simplicity. Tell us what works, what's confusing, and what feature would save you the most time. There's no such thing as small feedback — we act on it.

Thanks for being here.

The FindA.Sale team
support@finda.sale
