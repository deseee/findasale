═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
 WHAT TO LOOK FOR (COMMON ISSUES)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

DARK MODE:
  • Amber/golden text hard to read? (amber-600 on dark slate may fail WCAG)
  • Form input borders invisible?
  • Card backgrounds distinct from page background?
  • Status badges (SOLD/LIVE/AUCTION) readable on dark cards?

TIER GATING:
  • SIMPLE blocked from analytics, batch-ops, command-center, brand-kit, workspace?
  • PRO blocked from workspace only (shows TEAMS upsell)?
  • TEAMS has full access?
  • Upsells visible and on-brand?

NAV ITEM COUNT:
  • SIMPLE: ~4 items (Dashboard, Items, Reputation, Payout)
  • PRO: should be ≥ SIMPLE
  • TEAMS: should be PRO + 1 (Workspace)

ACCESSIBILITY:
  • TAB key navigates all interactive elements?
  • Focus states visible (blue outline)?
  • Icon-only buttons have aria-labels?
  • Mobile bottom nav icons labeled?

BADGES/CARDS:
  • Badge explosion? (multiple status badges stacking oddly on sale cards)
  • Images loading?
  • No broken 404s in console?
