There's also the time problem. Manual inventory lists — typed into spreadsheets, uploaded photo by photo, cross-posted to multiple platforms — take hours per sale. Hours you're not getting paid for.

### What Digital-First Estate Sales Actually Look Like

At FindA.Sale, we built a platform specifically for estate sale organizers in the Grand Rapids area. Here's how a typical sale flows:

**Photo → Item in 30 seconds.** Use the rapid batch upload tool: photograph items on your phone, upload in bulk, and our AI suggests titles, descriptions, categories, and a starting price. You review and confirm. What used to take hours takes minutes.

**Your sale, your pricing.** You set fixed prices or run mini-auctions on high-value items. Shoppers can reserve items before the sale starts, reducing no-show friction.

**5% platform fee — flat.** No percentage tiers, no subscription surprises. On that same $6,000 sale, you keep $5,700 instead of $4,800–$5,220.

### Shoppers Find You Automatically

Every sale you create gets a dedicated page with photos, categories, map, and dates. Shoppers in Grand Rapids who follow your profile get push notifications when you open a new sale. No more relying on Facebook algorithm changes.
