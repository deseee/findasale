# FindA.Sale Branding Brief
**Date:** 2026-03-05 | **Tier:** 2 — Load on demand during branding/design sessions

## Recommended Visual Identity

**Primary:** Warm Amber (#D97706) — discovery, vintage, warmth. Differentiates from competitor blues.
**Secondary:** Sage Green (#6B9E7F) — sustainability, calm, complements amber.
**Accent:** Deep Navy (#1F2937) — trust, contrast, form fields.
**Neutrals:** Off-white (#FAF8F6) light, dark slate (#1F2937) dark mode.

**Typography:** Serif headlines (Fraunces or Lora — free, Google Fonts) + Sans body (Inter or DM Sans). Serif conveys heritage; sans ensures mobile readability.

**Iconography:** Minimal line-drawn, 2px stroke, rounded corners. Feather Icons or Heroicons as base.

**Brand Voice:** Knowledgeable, friendly, enthusiastic, honest. Different tone for organizers (supportive, efficiency-focused) vs shoppers (playful, discovery-focused).

**Messaging Pillars:** Discovery ("Find your next treasure"), Trust ("Organizers you can count on"), Efficiency ("Faster sales, happier shoppers"), Community ("Real people, real finds"), Sustainability ("Give vintage a second life").

## Affordable Branding Path
- Phase 1 ($200–300): Looka/Hatchful exploration + Canva mood board
- Phase 2 ($1,200–1,500): Professional designer via 99designs or Dribbble
- Phase 3 ($500–800): Template expansion, social/email assets

## PWA-Specific
- App icon: Abstract mark, warm amber, recognizable at 32px
- manifest.json: theme_color #D97706, background_color #FAF8F6
- Splash: minimal, on-brand, warm palette

## Competitive Gap
EstateSales.NET = dated/functional. Poshmark/Depop = social/fashion. FindA.Sale can own: **premium + playful + purposeful** — modern design that respects the treasure hunt while giving organizers power tools.
