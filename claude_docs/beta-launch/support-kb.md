## Getting Started: Fastest Path to Your First Sale

**Q:** What's the quickest way to get my first sale live on the platform?

**A:** Create a sale (title, location, dates), add items with photos, and publish. You'll be live within an hour. Pro tip: use our AI photo analyzer when uploading items—it auto-fills title, description, and suggested price, saving 10+ minutes per item. All photos upload to Cloudinary in seconds.

## Payouts and Payment Processing

**Q:** When do I get paid, and how does it work?

**A:** We process payouts daily through Stripe Connect. Once a buyer pays, the sale amount (minus our 10% platform fee and Stripe's ~3.2% processing fee) hits your bank account the next business day. You control your Stripe Connect account, so you can check payout status anytime.

**Related:** Organizer dashboard → Payouts

## Fee Structure: What FindA.Sale Charges

**Q:** What does FindA.Sale charge, and how much will I actually keep?

**A:** We charge a flat 10% platform fee on all sales (fixed-price and auction). Combined with Stripe payment processing (roughly 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction), your all-in cost is about 13.2% per transaction. Here's an example: a $100 sale costs you ~$13.20, so you keep ~$86.80. That's still 3–4x cheaper than traditional estate sale companies (which charge 35–50%+).

## Photo Upload Requirements

**Q:** How do I add photos, and what formats or sizes work?

**A:** Upload JPEG, PNG, or WebP photos up to 5MB each. You can upload up to 20 photos at once for a sale, or one photo per item. We store them on Cloudinary and automatically create optimized versions for mobile and desktop. No extra work needed—just drag and drop.

## Cancelling or Ending a Sale Early

**Q:** Can I cancel my sale or end it early? What happens to bids and sales?

**A:** You can cancel a sale or deactivate it at any time from your organizer dashboard. Fixed-price sales that haven't sold are removed immediately. Active bids on auction items are cancelled, and bidders are notified. If items have already sold and payment cleared, those transactions stand—we can't reverse a completed purchase (though you can issue a refund).

**Related:** Organizer dashboard → Sale Settings

## Why My Sale Isn't Showing in Search

**Q:** My sale is published but isn't showing up in search. What's wrong?

**A:** Make sure your sale dates haven't ended—the platform only shows active sales. If it's active and still not visible, check that your neighborhood and address are filled in correctly (these help with map visibility). Sales typically appear in search within 1–2 minutes of publishing. If it's been longer, check for any validation errors on the sale page.

## Setting Up an Auction Item and Reserve Prices

**Q:** How do I set up an auction item? What's a reserve price, and do I need one?

**A:** On the item creation screen, choose "Auction" as the item type. Set a starting bid (the opening bid price) and optionally a reserve price (the minimum you'll accept to sell). If bidding doesn't reach your reserve, the item doesn't sell. Reserve prices help protect high-value items from selling below what you need. Without a reserve, the sale happens at whatever the final bid is—even if it's just the opening amount.

**Related:** Item creation guide (organizer help)

## Messaging Buyers and Arranging Pickup

**Q:** Can I message buyers after they purchase? How do I handle pickup logistics?

**A:** You can't send messages directly through FindA.Sale, but buyers receive a receipt email with their purchase details. You should provide pickup instructions in your sale description (date, time, location, parking, contact number). After a buyer wins or purchases an item, reach out using the contact info they provided at checkout. Clear pickup logistics up front to avoid confusion on sale day.

## Issuing a Refund

**Q:** How do I issue a refund if a buyer has a problem?

**A:** From your organizer dashboard, go to the purchase record and click "Issue Refund." The refund goes back to the buyer's original payment method (credit card, debit card, or PayPal) within 1–3 business days. The item becomes available for sale again. Keep in mind: once a refund is issued, you can't undo it, so confirm the buyer's issue first.

**Related:** Organizer dashboard → Purchases → Refund an item

## Account and Login Issues

**Q:** I can't log in or forgot my password. What do I do?

**A:** Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and check your inbox for a reset link (valid for 1 hour). If you don't see it, check spam. If you're still locked out, verify you're using the same email you signed up with. For other login issues (SSO problems, browser cache), try a different browser or device. Contact support if the reset link doesn't work.

## Mobile and Browser Compatibility

**Q:** Does FindA.Sale work on my phone? Any browser issues?

**A:** Yes—FindA.Sale is a progressive web app (PWA) that works on any modern phone or tablet. Use the latest version of Chrome, Safari, Firefox, or Edge for the best experience. If you're having issues, clear your browser cache and reload. You can also add FindA.Sale to your home screen on iOS or Android for faster access.
