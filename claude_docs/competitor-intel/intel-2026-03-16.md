## Competitor Updates

**EstateSales.org** — No changes detected. Status quo.

**Blue Moon / Moonetize™ / Valuable — ROLLOUT COMPLETE:** The AI partnership that launched Feb 3, 2026 with 30 pilot franchisees has now **fully deployed across all remaining franchisees**. All 156 Blue Moon locations are now running Valuable's AI for photo editing, inventory, pricing research, item descriptions, and marketing. The franchise AI moat is now closed. FindA.Sale's differentiator must be framed around independent operators — a segment Blue Moon explicitly does not serve.

**PROSALE (watch)** — Surfaced this week as a direct competitor to FindA.Sale's organizer tooling. "First in Class" estate sale management software with POS, inventory, mobile label printing, integrated payments, websites with online stores, and suggestive pricing. Web-based. Worth monitoring — this is the incumbent organizer software FindA.Sale is disrupting.

Blue Moon published a franchise recruitment push titled "Why Estate Sale Franchises Are a Smart Investment in 2026" — the big franchise money is consolidating.
