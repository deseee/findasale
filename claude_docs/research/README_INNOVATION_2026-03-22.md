## Key Insights Across All Topics

1. **Trust is currency in marketplaces.** Reputation systems (BizBuySell), transparency (Joybird), data verification (Acquire) all drive conversion.
   → FindA.Sale's P0: Ship reputation + condition transparency early.

2. **Furniture is high-friction, high-ROI.** Joybird's patterns (imagery, AR, expert support) directly apply to estate sales.
   → 30–40% of estate items are furniture; room visualization could be category differentiator.

3. **Digital assets are emerging market with massive TAM.** 90% of crypto holders have no inheritance plan; gaming accounts trade for $1K–$50K+.
   → Low-risk entry: inventory tab + legal review. High-risk: marketplace (defer until legal clarity).

4. **Print materials are quick monetization.** Estate organizers spend 4–8 hours on manual printing; Printful can automate → 20% margin.
   → Print Kit is best risk/reward ratio of all revenue features.

5. **B2B opportunities emerge from mastering B2C.** Once FindA.Sale has rich data (comparables, reputation, pricing), estate attorneys + brokers become customers.
   → Comparables search (Q4 2026) is prerequisite for attorney/broker B2B (2027).
