## Explorer's Guild Design Decisions — 2026-04-22

### Grandmaster Hunt Pass Duration (S544 — revises Phase 2 locked decision)

**DECISION:** Free Hunt Pass is tied to active Grandmaster status (≥12,000 XP). Benefit is granted while the user holds Grandmaster rank. On Jan 1 reset, if they drop to Sage soft floor (5,000 XP), the free Hunt Pass lapses. Re-achieving Grandmaster (≥12,000 XP) restores it. Users must re-qualify annually.

**PLAYER EXPERIENCE:** Grandmaster feels like an annual title you defend, not a lifetime badge you pocket — the free pass is the reward for staying elite.

**RATIONALE:** "Free forever" (previous Phase 2 decision) undermines the seasonal reset's core retention mechanic for the highest-value users. Annual re-qualification (7,000 XP re-grind from Sage floor to GM) keeps Grandmasters active year-round and aligns the benefit with sustained effort, not a one-time achievement. Lapsed GMs who don't re-qualify also become paying Hunt Pass subscribers again.

**IMPACT:** MEDIUM positive on annual retention of top users; SMALL positive on revenue from lapsed GMs; LOW negative on aspiration (GM still feels elite — the benefit is still the best in the system).
