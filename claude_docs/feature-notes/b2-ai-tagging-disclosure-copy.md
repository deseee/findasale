# B2: AI Tagging Disclosure Copy

## 1. Disclosure Tooltip Copy
**Placement:** Appears on hover/tap next to AI sparkle icon on tagged items in organizer dashboard and public item listings.

**Copy:**
"This tag was auto-suggested by our system. Check it before listing — you know your items best."

---

## 2. Organizer Onboarding Disclosure
**Placement:** Helper note in "Add Items" flow, shown on first use of AI tagging feature.

**Copy:**
"We can auto-suggest categories, tags, and descriptions for your items — it's a quick way to get started. Just review what we suggest before you publish. You're always in control of what shows on your listings, and you can edit or remove anything."

---

## 3. Shopper-Facing Disclosure
**Placement:** Item detail page, shown once per item when any field was AI-generated (e.g., below the description or near the tags).

**Copy:**
"Some of this item's details were auto-suggested — check with the seller if you have questions."

---

## 4. Settings/Preferences Copy
**Placement:** "AI Assistance" toggle in organizer account settings.

**Copy:**
"Let our system auto-suggest tags and descriptions when you add items. We'll always flag what's auto-suggested so you can review and change it. You can turn this off anytime."

---

## Notes
- All copy avoids jargon ("machine learning," "algorithm," etc.) and speaks plainly.
- Emphasizes human control and review — organizers remain the authority on their inventory.
- Shopper copy is minimal; most shoppers won't read it, but it's there for transparency and to reduce friction if a description seems off.
- Tone is reassuring without being defensive or over-explaining.
